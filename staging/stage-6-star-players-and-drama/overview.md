# Stage 6 — Star players & drama

## Goal
Deepen fandom: turn on ~4 named star players per team (the slot the data model has held since Stage 3), track scorers/streaks/rivalries, generate richer storyline captions, and publish a public standings/records page linked in the IG bio.

## Why this stage exists
Named competitors + underdog/streak arcs + a home for fans between drops are high-leverage fandom drivers (research §5, P1). The user tabled players for v1 but kept the door open — research flags them as worth turning on relatively early.

## Features
- `feature-star-players.md` — named stars, scorers, per-player stats/personas.
- `feature-public-standings-page.md` — public web page: live table, team/player records, head-to-head.

## Definition of done
Captions and standings reference named players and storylines (scorers, streaks, rivalries); a public standings/records page is live and linked in the bio.

## Blocked by
Stage 3 data model (players slot) exists; best after at least Soccer is live (Stages 1–4).
